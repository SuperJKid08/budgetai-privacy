Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my bank account?

Go to Settings → Connect Bank Account. You'll be redirected to Plaid Link, a secure service that connects your bank account. Follow the prompts to select your bank and sign in.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption and security. Your bank credentials are stored securely and encrypted. We use Plaid, which is trusted by millions of users and major financial institutions.

How does AI categorization work?

Our AI analyzes your transaction details (merchant name, amount, date) and automatically categorizes them. You can always manually change the category by tapping on any transaction.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Settings and you'll find an option to delete your account. This will remove all your data permanently.

How do I set up budgets?

You can create budgets through the Budget tab or by chatting with the AI assistant. Simply say "Create a budget for groceries" or similar.

Why am I not receiving push notifications?

Make sure you've enabled notifications in your iOS Settings → BudgetAI → Notifications. Also ensure you've connected at least one bank account so we can detect new transactions.
